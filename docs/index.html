Understanding Sustainable Production and Livelihoods -

Sustainable Production: The creation of goods and services using processes that are non-polluting, conserve energy and natural resources, and are economically viable.

Livelihoods: The means by which individuals secure the necessities of life, including food, water, shelter and income.
